Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and respect your privacy

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At IDLab, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our ID card creation software. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

Data You Provide:

  • Student/employee information entered into the software
  • Photos uploaded for ID card creation
  • School or business profile information
  • Custom templates and design preferences
  • Feedback and support communications

Technical Information:

  • Software version and update history
  • License activation and validation data
  • Error logs and crash reports (if you opt in)
  • Basic usage statistics (anonymized)

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal photos or student data during normal use
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Financial or payment information (handled by payment processors)
  • Location data or device identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

Local Processing:

Most data processing happens locally on your device:

  • ID card generation and template application
  • Photo processing and layout creation
  • Data import/export operations
  • Print formatting and PDF generation

Limited Cloud Services:

We only use cloud services for:

  • Software updates and security patches
  • License validation and activation
  • Optional backup services (encrypted)
  • Technical support (with your consent)

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage:

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device
  • Photos and student information never leave your computer during normal use
  • You have complete control over data retention and deletion
  • Local data is protected by your device's security measures

Cloud Backup (Optional):

  • Encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption
  • Only accessible with your unique encryption key
  • Stored securely with reputable cloud providers
  • Can be deleted at any time upon request

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may only share data in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers:

  • Cloud storage providers (encrypted data only)
  • Payment processors for license purchases
  • Technical support services (with your explicit consent)

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or prevent harm
  • In case of business transfer (with continued privacy protection)

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Export your data in common formats
  • Opt-out: Decline optional data collection
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

7. Children's Privacy

IDLab is often used to create student ID cards, which may include information about minors:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Schools and institutions are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's information
  • All processing must comply with educational privacy laws (FERPA, etc.)

8. International Data Transfers

If you use optional cloud features:

  • Data may be processed in countries outside Zambia
  • We ensure adequate protection through contractual safeguards
  • Encryption protects data during transfer and storage
  • You can opt out of cloud features at any time

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our desktop software does not use cookies or web tracking. Our website may use:

  • Essential cookies for basic functionality
  • Analytics cookies to improve our website (anonymized)
  • No advertising or behavioral tracking cookies
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings

10. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary:

  • Local data: Until you delete it
  • License information: For the duration of your license
  • Support communications: 2 years after resolution
  • Backup data: Until you request deletion
  • Analytics data: 2 years maximum (anonymized)

11. Security Measures

We implement various security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for cloud services
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Secure communication protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and testing

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements:

  • We will notify users of significant changes
  • Updates will be posted on our website
  • Continued use implies acceptance of updates
  • You can always review the current version online
