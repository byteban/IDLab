Core Features

Data Management

Create, import, and manage student records with CSV import/export. Search and filter by name, exam number, or grade level with category-based organization.

Photo Management

Smart photo matching system with renaming wizard. Track photo status and handle bulk uploads efficiently for large student databases.

ID Card Design

Customizable templates with school branding options. Add logos, coat of arms, custom colors, school motto, and design both front and back sides.

Output Options

Generate high-quality PDFs for multiple cards, export individual images, preview print layouts, and create separate back-side PDFs.

Batch Processing

Generate hundreds of cards at once with consistent quality. Perfect for schools processing entire grades or large organizations.

Local-First Security

All data processed locally on your computer. No student information sent to external servers, ensuring privacy and compliance.
