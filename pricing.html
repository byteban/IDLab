Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for my license?

You can pay via Airtel Money (+260 777 274 339) or bank transfer. After payment, upload a screenshot as proof and we'll process your license within 24 hours. if you have questions feel free to reach out. Email: contact@idlab.studio

What's included with my license?

All licenses include unlimited candidate/client processing, access to all templates, CSV import/export, and email support. Higher tiers include additional features like phone support and training.

Can I use IDLab for commercial purposes?

Yes! Business licenses include commercial usage rights, perfect for stationery shops and print resellers offering ID card services to schools.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with IDLab. Contact us within 7 days of license activation for a full refund.
