About IDLab

Building practical, accessible identity solutions for real institutions.

Our Mission

We created IDLab to remove friction from issuing ID cards. Many schools and small organizations rely on manual layouts or outdated tools. IDLab streamlines the process: structured data in → polished ID cards out. Fast, consistent, and secure.

We focus on simplicity first. Every feature must save time or reduce mistakes. As the project grows we aim to support smart credentials, secure QR features, and broader integrations.

What We Value

  • Practical Design: Features shaped by real user needs.
  • Reliability: Outputs you can trust for official use.
  • Performance: Handle large batches without slowing down.
  • Accessibility: Usable by non‑technical staff.
  • Privacy: Local-first profile storage.
