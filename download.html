Frequently Asked Questions

Is IDLab really free?

Yes! IDLab Desktop is completely free for personal and commercial use. No hidden fees or subscriptions.

Can I use it offline?

Absolutely. IDLab works completely offline. Internet is only needed for downloading updates or cloud sync features.

What file formats can I import?

Import CSV, Excel files, or manually enter data. Photos can be JPG, PNG, or BMP format.

How many cards can I create?

No limits! Create as many ID cards as you need. Perfect for schools, offices, or events.
