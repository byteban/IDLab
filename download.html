Common Questions

Do I need both apps?

Desktop is required for card creation. Android is optional—use it if you need to capture photos on-site with a mobile device.

Can I use Desktop without Android?

Yes. You can add photos directly to Desktop from any camera or device. Android just makes on-site capture easier.

What file formats are supported?

Import: CSV, Excel. Photos: JPG, PNG, BMP. Export: PDF, PNG, JPG.

Is it really free?

Yes. Both apps are completely free for personal and commercial use with no hidden fees.
